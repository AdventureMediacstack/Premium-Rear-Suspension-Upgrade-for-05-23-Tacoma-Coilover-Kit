Product Information

Our coilover lift kit includes oversized 2.5″ shock bodies designed for superior oil capacity and heat dissipation key for maintaining performance during long trail runs or heavy load use. These shocks are engineered to perform under pressure, whether you're navigating technical off-road terrain or cruising on pavement.

Each component is built for serious durability: black zinc‑coated steel shock bodies resist corrosion, 4130 chromed shafts ensure strength and smooth operation, Buna seals offer lasting leak protection, and anodized 6061 aluminum parts provide both strength and reduced weight. This system delivers long-lasting reliability, even under punishing conditions, something off-roaders have consistently praised on platforms like AccuTune Off-Road and Taco Clout.

The shocks feature 22-position compression adjusters and finned remote reservoirs, giving you precision control over ride feel and heat management. Compared to stock suspension, our kit delivers up to 25% more front wheel travel, unlocking smoother landings, better traction, and a more planted feel on uneven terrain.

Designed as a 100% bolt‑on fit for 2005–2023 Toyota Tacomas, installation requires no welding or cutting, just real, functional performance right out of the box.
